Animals in the Sahara Desert by Jonathan Levy

Fennec Fox

This is a Fennec Fox. It can survive in high temperature, low water enviornments and eats mainly insects, small mamals, and birds.

Hyrax

Hyraxes are mamals that are herbavors. They like huddling together and basking in the sun. In this picture the Hyrax does not look happy.

Lesser Egyptian Jerboa

These small bipedal mammal have elongate hindlimbs that let them jump up to 10 times thier hip height. They eat seeds and insects in the Sahara.
